Technology we used in our BIB Trolley

The 'Built-in Billing Trolley' utilizes advanced technology, including sensors like IR sensors, Arduino uno, Barcode scanner, Computer vision and Software website (which you are using), to streamline the shopping experience. Sensors embedded in the trolley track the items placed inside, automatically updating the cart's contents and calculating the total bill. The trolley is connected to a user-friendly software billing system, accessible via a website or mobile app, allowing customers to easily view and manage their purchases. This innovative technology enables seamless and efficient checkout processes, reducing wait times and enhancing customer satisfaction. Additionally, the software provides valuable insights for retailers, enabling them to optimize inventory management, track sales trends, and improve overall store operations.
